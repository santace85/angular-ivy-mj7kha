This test is meant to evaluate your ability to perform common JavaScript/Angular operations while showcasing your unique creative flair.

You are encouraged to utilize common Angular methods & services for this test, but feel free to express yourself and your creative abilities.

Your task is to create a list page that displays an array of movies from IMDB and:

  • Include the following info on the list: "title", "year", "director", "genre", "score"
  • Make the list sortable by "title", "year", "director", and "score"
  • Include a string search filter for "title", "year", "director", and "score"
  • Have some way to add/remove movies from the list. You may use the additional movie list supplied or add a movie in manually.
